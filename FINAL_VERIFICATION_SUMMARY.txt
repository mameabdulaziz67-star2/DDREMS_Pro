  ✅ Endpoint: PUT /api/agreement-workflow/:agreementId/forward-to-owner
  ✅ Status: waiting_owner_response

STEP 3: Owner Decides
  ✅ Endpoint: PUT /api/agreement-workflow/:agreementId/owner-decision
  ✅ Status: owner_accepted or owner_rejected

  ✅ Endpoint: POST /api/agreement-workflow/:agreementId/generate-agreement
  ✅ Status: agreement_generated
  ✅ AUTO-POPULATE TRIGGERED HERE ⭐

  ✅ Status: customer_editing

STEP 6: Customer Submits Agreement
  ✅ Endpoint: POST /api/agreement-workflow/:agreementId/submit-agreement
  ✅ Status: customer_submitted

  ✅ Endpoint: PUT /api/agreement-workflow/:agreementId/admin-review
  ✅ Status: waiting_owner_final_review

╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Step 1: User sees sidebar with new "⚡ AI Advice" button
        ↓
Step 2: User clicks "⚡ AI Advice" button
        ↓
Step 3: Modal opens with AI Advice panel
        ↓
Step 4: User sees "✨ Get Advice" button
        ↓
Step 5: User clicks "✨ Get Advice"
        ↓
Step 6: Loading state shows "⏳ Loading..."
        ↓
Step 7: Backend processes role and generates advice
        ↓
Step 8: Frontend displays:
        • Title and description
        • Recommendations list
        • Key metrics
        • Important alerts
        ↓
Step 9: User can:
        • Read recommendations
        • View metrics
        • Click "🔄 Refresh" for updated advice
        • Click "✕" to close modal

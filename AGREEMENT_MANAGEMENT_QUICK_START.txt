================================================================================
                         HOW TO USE
================================================================================

STEP 1: Restart Backend
  npm start

STEP 2: Login as Customer
  - Click "📋 Agreement Management" button (top of page)
  - View all your agreements
  - Click "💳 Submit Payment" to submit payment
  - Click "📄 Upload Receipt" to upload receipt
  - Notifications sent to admin automatically

STEP 3: Login as Admin
  - Click "📋 Agreement Management" button
  - View all pending agreements
  - Click "📄 Generate Agreement" to generate
  - Click "📧 Send Agreement" to send to customer/owner
  - Click "🔔 Send Notification" to send custom message
  - All parties receive notifications

STEP 4: View Notifications
  - Check agreement_notifications table
  - All actions create notifications
  - Notifications appear in real-time

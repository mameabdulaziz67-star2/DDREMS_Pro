                        🆕 HOW TO ADD NEW PROPERTIES WITH IMAGES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Login as Broker (john@ddrems.com) or Owner (owner@ddrems.com)

STEP 2: Click "Add New Property" button

STEP 3: Fill in property details:
        - Title, Type, Price, Location, etc.
        - Click "Next: Upload Images"

STEP 4: Upload images:
        - Click to select image files from your computer
        - Select JPG, PNG, or other image files
        - First image becomes the main image
        - Click "Upload X Image(s)"

STEP 5: Upload documents (optional):
        - Click "Next: Preview & Submit"

STEP 6: Preview your listing:
        - See all uploaded images
        - Review property details
        - Click "Submit Property for Approval"

STEP 7: Admin approval:
        - Login as admin@ddrems.com
        - Go to "Property Approval" section
        - Approve the property

STEP 8: View as customer:
        - Login as customer@ddrems.com
        - See your new property with real images!
